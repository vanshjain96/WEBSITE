Services on Sentinel

Sentinel VPN

On the Sentinel Network runs a dVPN (decentralized Virtual Private Network) that runs on a network of nodes from around the world and provide bandwidth to enable secure access of information and services.

Sentinel VOIP

The Sentinel Network offers a dVoIP (decentralized Voice over Internet) service for secure access of audio communication between various devices.

dVoIP service is powered by a decentralized network that contributes bandwidth and powers the VoIP engine.

Sentinel dChat

The Sentinel Network runs dChat, a decentralized peer to peer messaging system that enables exchange of messages and files between various clients available on multiple devices

Put together, the Sentinel Communication Suite enables secure access of information and content from across the web, enables peer-to-peer communication and exchange of media & files

All this runs on a network of decentralized nodes communicating between each other to runs engines and databases of the app along with another network of nodes that contributes bandwidth and resources for the functioning of the network

Hardware

.

Sentinels decentralized VPN Network will allow users to monetize on their unused bandwidth and act as a relay in the network or as an exit node.

A relay or an exit node must be on systems with high bandwidth connection with little downtime during when the user intends to participant as a service provider in the system.

Sentinels VPN Hardware solution is a prototype of a quintessential in-home network security solution which would allow users to connect to their personal secured VPN exit point regardless of wherever they are in the world. The docker image hosted on the raspberry-pi node will be accessible directly from the mobile app and this hardware solution can be placed in close proximity to the users router acting as a low-energy consuming 24/7 uptime secured node.

At this point in time sentinel would encourage users to build their own nodes and even commercialize them if they market demands it, however there will be a free airdrop to active users in order to bolster community engagement.
